Instructions

The objective of this game is to memorise sequences of sights and sounds. Every three seconds you will see a square appear in one of eight possible locations and hear one of eight possible letters. You have to remember whether any of the following have repeated:

  • only the location of the square (visual).
  • only the sound (audio).
  • both the location of the square and the sound (both).

When the game starts you have to identify where any of the visual, audio, or both, have repeated 1 interval ago. So, in the above example, you can see that:

  • the visual has repeated between the first and second intervals.
  • the audio has repeated between the second and third intervals.

After every twenty intervals, the size of the interval within which you have to memorise the visual and audio targets may change, depending on your performance.
